About The Founder

Dr. Sudeep Satpathy is a sports medicine physician from Odisha, India. He holds an MBBS from VSS Medical College Burla, an MD from SCB Medical College, Cuttack, and a Diploma in sports medicine from the International Olympic Committee. He is a professor at the Kalinga Institute of Medical Sciences (KIMS), Bhubaneswar, where he serves as the Head of the Physiology and Sports Medicine Department. Dr. Satpathy has an extensive history of working with elite sports organizations, including serving as the team doctor for the Indian Men’s Hockey Team for the Tokyo Olympics 2021, the first doctor from Odisha to receive this honor. He has also been involved in the Rio Olympics 2016, working in the polyclinic and Athletes Park, and has served as a tournament doctor for major events like the BWF World Championships, FIFA World Cup Qualifiers, AFC Champions League, Premier Badminton League and acted as a Doping Control Officer for the AFC Champions League-West 2020.
