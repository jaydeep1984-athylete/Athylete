About The Founder

Dr. Sudeep Satpathy is a dynamic healthcare professional, academician, and sports medicine specialist with extensive experience in advancing medical care, athlete performance, and health innovation. With a strong background in sports and healthcare entrepreneurship, he has contributed significantly to clinical practice, research, and institutional development. He currently serves as Head of Sports and Exercise Science at the Khelo India State Centre of Excellence (KISCE), Kalinga Stadium, Bhubaneswar, where he leads initiatives in athlete health, performance optimization, and injury prevention. His expertise also extends to academic research and an upcoming Ph.D. on Artificial Intelligence in Predicting Sports Injuries and Enhancing Performance. As the founder of ATHYLETE, a brand dedicated to sports medicine, fitness, and scientific athlete care, Dr. Satpathy continues to bridge the gap between evidence-based medicine and performance optimization. His upcoming books – “The Career Coumpounding,” “The Memory Dividend,” and “The Beauty of Less” – reflect his philosophy of continuous growth, mindful living, and maximizing human potential. Driven by a passion for sports science, medical innovation, and athlete wellness, Dr. Satpathy combines clinical expertise, research acumen, and visionary leadership to make a lasting impact on healthcare and sports performance in India and abroad
